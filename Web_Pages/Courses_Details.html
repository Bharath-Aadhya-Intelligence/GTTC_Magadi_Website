Diploma Courses

Diploma in AI and ML (DAIML)

Introduction

The Diploma in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (DAIML) is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in AI and ML concepts, techniques, and tools. This program equips students with the necessary knowledge and practical skills required to implement AI and ML algorithms in real-world applications across various sectors.

Job Placement Opportunities

  • AI Engineer / Machine Learning Engineer
  • Data Scientist / Data Analyst
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) Specialist
  • Robotics Engineer
  • AI Consultant in finance and healthcare

Salary Expectations

  • Entry-Level: ₹4,00,000 - ₹8,00,000 PA
  • Mid-Level: ₹8,00,000 - ₹15,00,000 PA

Diploma in Tool and Die Making (DTDM)

Introduction

Tool and Die Making is a specialized branch of manufacturing engineering involved in designing and maintaining tools, dies, molds, and special equipment. Critical for mass production in automotive, aerospace, and electronics industries.

Job Placement Opportunities

  • Automotive Industry: Maruti Suzuki, Tata Motors, Toyota
  • Aerospace Sector: HAL, Boeing, Airbus
  • Electronics: Samsung, LG, Bosch
  • Typical Roles: Tool Maker, CNC Operator, Quality Control

Salary Expectations

  • Training: ₹15,000 - ₹20,000 PM
  • Entry-Level: ₹2.4L - ₹4.8L PA

Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (DEEE)

Introduction

Focused on equipping students with in-depth knowledge of electrical systems, electronics circuits, and automation technology. Graduates contribute to power generation, telecommunications, and manufacturing sectors.

Job Placement Opportunities

  • Power Generation and Distribution Companies
  • Telecommunication Industry (Network Design & Management)
  • Automation and Robotics Firms
  • Typical Roles: Electrical Engineer, Electronics Technician, Power Plant Operator

Diploma in Mechatronics (DMCH)

Introduction

A multidisciplinary program combining mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. Focuses on automation, robotics, control systems, and advanced manufacturing processes (Industry 4.0).

Job Placement Opportunities

  • Automotive: Tesla, BMW, Volvo
  • Robotics: ABB, Fanuc
  • Electronics: Siemens, Panasonic
  • Typical Roles: Mechatronics Technician, Automation Engineer

Salary Expectations

  • Entry-Level: ₹20,000 - ₹35,000 PM
  • Mid-Level: ₹40,000 - ₹60,000 PM

About GTTC

Government Tool Room and Training Centre (GTTC) was established in 1972 with the participation of the Karnataka State Government and the Government of Denmark. It provides high-quality technical education and skill development.
