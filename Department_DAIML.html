About the Department

The Diploma in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (DAIML) is designed to provide students with a solid foundation in modern computational intelligence. The program covers essential concepts, techniques, and tools needed to build "smart" systems that can learn from data.


In the digital age, AI and ML are transforming sectors from healthcare to finance. At GTTC Magadi, we focus on equipping students with practical skills in data processing, algorithm implementation, and real-world AI applications, ensuring they are ready for the future of technology.

Infrastructure & Laboratories

The DAIML department features advanced computing facilities for data-intensive training:


  • AI & Computing Lab: High-performance workstations with GPU acceleration.
  • Data Science Lab: Specialized software for statistical analysis and big data processing.
  • Programming Lab: Dedicated environment for Python, R, and Java development.
  • IoT & Robotics Lab: Integrating AI with physical sensors and automated devices.
