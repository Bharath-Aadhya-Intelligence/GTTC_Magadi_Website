About the Department

The Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (DEEE) is a comprehensive technical program focusing on equipping students with in-depth knowledge of electrical systems, electronic circuits, and automation technology.


Electrical and electronics engineers play a crucial role in industries that design, implement, and maintain the infrastructure of modern life. At GTTC Magadi, our DEEE program blends core electrical principles with the latest electronics and power electronics applications to prepare students for the global industry.

Infrastructure & Laboratories

The DEEE department provides specialized labs for power and electronics training:


  • Electrical Machines Lab: DC motors, transformers, and AC alternators.
  • Power Electronics Lab: Thyristor and converter-based control systems.
  • Analog & Digital Electronics Lab: Circuit design and testing benches.
  • Switchgear & Protection Lab: High-voltage testing and relay systems.

About GTTC

Government Tool Room and Training Centre (GTTC) was established in 1972 with the participation of the Karnataka State Government and the Government of Denmark. It provides high-quality technical education and skill development.
