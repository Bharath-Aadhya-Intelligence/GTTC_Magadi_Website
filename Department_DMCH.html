About the Department

The Diploma in Mechatronics is a multidisciplinary program that combines mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering. This branch equips students with cutting-edge knowledge in automation, robotics, control systems, and advanced manufacturing processes.


Mechatronics plays a vital role in modern industries like automotive, aerospace, electronics, and industrial automation. The program at GTTC Magadi focuses on providing a holistic understanding of how these engineering fields intersect to create "smart" systems and machines.

Infrastructure & Laboratories

The DMCH department features state-of-the-art labs for multi-disciplinary training:


  • Robotics Lab: Hands-on experience with industrial robot arms and cobots.
  • PLC & Automation Lab: Modern PLC systems (Siemens/Mitsubishi) and sensors.
  • Pneumatics & Hydraulics Lab: Fluid power control systems.
  • Embedded Systems Lab: Microcontrollers, Arduino, and IoT applications.

About GTTC

Government Tool Room and Training Centre (GTTC) was established in 1972 with the participation of the Karnataka State Government and the Government of Denmark. It provides high-quality technical education and skill development.
